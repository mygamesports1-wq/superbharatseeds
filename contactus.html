FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our seeds, ordering, and support.

How do I order seeds from Super Bharat Seeds Company? +
You can contact us through this form, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp. Our team will guide you through available varieties, quantities, and pricing for your specific requirements.
Are your seeds government certified? +
Yes. All our seed varieties are certified by the National Seeds Corporation (NSC) and relevant State Seed Certification Agencies. Certification documents are available on request.
Do you supply seeds in bulk for dealers? +
Absolutely. We have a dedicated dealer and distributor program across North India. Please use the contact form above or select "Bulk Order / Dealer Enquiry" in the subject to get started.
Which states do you supply seeds to? +
We currently supply seeds across Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Punjab. We are expanding to more states — contact us for current availability in your region.
Can I download seed brochures? +
Yes. You can download our Wheat Seed and Barley Seed brochures directly from the navigation bar or from the Products section on our homepage. They contain full variety details and specifications.
Do you offer agronomy support after purchase? +
Yes. Our team of agronomists is available to support you before sowing, during the season, and post-harvest. Call us or WhatsApp us for any technical guidance related to our seed varieties.
